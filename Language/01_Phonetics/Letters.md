# The Alphabet of Lingua Sona
## Letter Counts
There are ***251*** letters in Lingua Sona's alphabet. These letters are composed of two (usually) or more [[Glyphs]] which help to massively reduce the learning complexity. 

---
## Structure
The letters in Lingua Sona follow a **phonetic** structure. Usually this is a consonant followed by a vowel. Though a few variations exist. 

---
## Imaginary Middle Binding Point
Letters are depicted by using a glyph for the consonant or a [[Negated Letter Glyph]] followed by a glyph for the vowel/vowel-cluster or a [[Negated Letter Glyph]]. To reduce the number of strokes used to depict each letter, you can imagine a, well, imaginary binding point in the middle of each little where the [[Glyphs]] connect together to tie into a letter.
