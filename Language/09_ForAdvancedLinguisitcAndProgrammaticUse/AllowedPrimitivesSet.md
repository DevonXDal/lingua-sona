# The Allowed Primitives
These primitives are one letter words and can be repeated or stack with each other intentionally for the creation of other words, as part of the terms themselves and not needing to be a part of a compound term.

| Primitive | Domain | Notes |
|---------|------|------|
| Happy | Positive affect | Baseline pleasure / contentment |
| Sad | Negative affect | Loss, absence, downturn |
| Calm | Arousal state | Low activation, safety |
| Fear | Survival affect | Threat detection |
| Anger | Boundary affect | Violation, injustice |
| Fun | Play affect | Voluntary enjoyment |
| Love | Bond affect | Attachment without obligation |
| Pain | Aversion | Physical & emotional |
| Want | Motivation | Desire without moral loading |
| Know | Cognitive affect | Resolution, clarity, understanding |
